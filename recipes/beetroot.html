Beetroot Salad

Description

Light and easy to make beetroot salad for hot summer days

Ingridients

  • Lettuce or Beetroot Leaves
  • Boiled beetroots
  • Creamy Goat Cheese
  • Cranberries
  • Pumpkin Seeds
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Wash lettuce of beetroot leaves.
  2. Slice the beetroot into.
  3. Put everything in a bowl.

And Voila! Your salad is ready!
