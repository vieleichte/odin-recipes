Tomato-Mozzarella Snack

Description

When it is tomato season, here's an option for you how you can consume many tomatoes and enjoy it!

Ingridients

  • Tomatoes!
  • Mozzarella (or Burrata)
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Salt
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut tomatoes to bite size.
  2. Slice mozzarella.
  3. Serve on a big plate or a cutting board.
  4. Add basil leaves.
  5. Drizzle balsamic vinegar on mozzarella.
  6. Sprinkle tomatoes with salt and drizzle with some olive oil.

And Voila! Your tomato snack is ready!
